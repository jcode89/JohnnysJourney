About Me

About Me

I am Jonathan Colon, born and raised in New York and a lover of a knowledge. I went to school for Political Science, and even graduated with a degree in that major, but along the way I found I love programming just as much as Political Science. I started out with C++ but switched to Python so I could get a better understanding of computer science. This choice was nothing but beneficial. I have learned so much, and continue to learn so much everyday while programming in Python. However, I don't want to limit myself to one language. Thus, I started learning C and Clojure as well. It isn't always easy learning a new language, but once you get a grasp of the syntax, the possibilities of what you can create seem endless. That is what I love most about programming, the endless possibilities.

My goal is to become a professional developer, but along the way I am going to post about my journey. I want to share my experiences with the world, and this seems the best way to do that.
